About Us

EBENEZER HIGHER INSTITUTE OF
SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

Ebenezer higher Institute of Science and Technology based in Bamenda, North West Region of Cameroon was founded at the peak of the Anglophone crisis and war to meet up with the educational and moral needs of students based on ó instruction. The crisis which led to the shutdown of schools, led to so many dropouts, increase in crime rates, prostitution and Gender based violence due to economic meltdown. Hence, Ebenezer higher institute of Science and Technology was the answer to the people’s cry because it offered quality professional education where there is a perfect blend of theory and practical at a very affordable cost and structured extracurricular programs which help to build the morals and character of students.

runs various programs authorized by the Ministry of Higher Education and the Ministry of Employment and Vocational Training for the National Diploma (ND) as well as supervised programs such as bachelor’s and Master’s degree with the University of Bamenda, Cameroon. The various programs offered include school of medical and biomedical sciences, school of engineering, school of management, school of business and finance, legal career, school of home economics and school of education.



Our Programs


School of Health and Biomedical Sciences

Embark on a journey of caring and discovery, mastering the science and art of health in diverse fields like nursing, midwifery, and medical laboratory sciences.


School of Computer Engineering and Networking

Weave code into reality, build intelligent systems, and connect the world through our dynamic programs in computer engineering and networking.

School of Business and Finance

Shape your future as a leader in the dynamic world of business and finance. Master the art of deal-making, navigate financial markets, and propel your career through our diverse and industry-aligned programs.


School of Management

Lead with vision and purpose, transforming organizations and shaping success through our dynamic programs in management, project management, human resources, and logistics & transport management

School of Law

Champion the law, advocate for justice, and navigate the intricate legal landscape through our comprehensive program in legal assisting.

School of Agriculture

Cultivate the future of food and agriculture through our dynamic programs in agricultural engineering, animal production, crop production, and agribusiness.


School of Education

Shape the future of education through our diverse programs in vocational guidance and counseling, didactics, curriculum development and teaching, and distance and continuing education.
